Manning Clark House

High Court

Rachel Jackson and ACT Government Architect Catherine Townsend discuss the High Court, listed on the National Heritage List.

Episode 12

Former Carousel Restaurant

Amy Jarvis discusses the Canberra landmark with a stunning view of the city and an interesting design story with links to European migration history.

Episode 13

Cinema Centre

Edwina Jans & CMAG's Virginia Rigney discuss the important contribution the Centre made to the cultural life of Canberra during the late twentieth century.

Episode 14

Canberra Bus Shelters

Discussing everything from bus to postcards and they are also talking about the distinctive Canberra icon with Georgia Stynes.

Episode 15

The Shine Dome

The Australian Academy of Science's Shine Dome was designed by prominent architect Roy Grounds and is listed on the National Heritage List. Rachel Jackson discusses this modernist icon with Emeritus Professor Hans Bachin.

Episode 16

Benjamin House

The Benjamin House, or Round House, is a residence in the Canberra suburb of Deakin. Army resident and former National President of the Australian Institute of Architects Roger Benjamin discusses this residential gem.

Scrivener Dam

A brutal and infrastructural piece of Canberra, Scrivener Dam helped shape the cultural landscape. Edwina Jans discusses the extraordinary story of the innovation and ingenuity of the men who built it.
